About Me

I grew up in Milwaukee, but lived in Colorado from 2013-2018. I'm now back home and studying web development at devCodeCamp.

I've worked for craft breweries in Colorado and Wisconsin. My favorite aspect of the job was identifying subtle flavor components and hunting for off-flavors to create a better product. Living out west instilled a love of the outdoors in me, and I go hiking and camping with my wife whenever I can. I've also been involved in charitable work with the Rocky Mountain Beard and Moustache Club in Fort Collins, and took home several trophies and facial hair competitions.
