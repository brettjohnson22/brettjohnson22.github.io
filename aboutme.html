About Me



I grew up in Milwaukee, but lived in Colorado from 2013-2018. I'm now back home and studying web development at devCodeCamp. I live in the vibrant Bay View Neighborhood on the shore of Lake Michigan.

Living out west instilled a love of the outdoors in me, and I go hiking and camping with my wife whenever I can. I've also been involved in charitable work with the Rocky Mountain Beard and Moustache Club in Fort Collins, raising money for the community by organizing and competing in facial hair competitions.

Previously, I've worked for craft breweries in Colorado and Wisconsin. My favorite aspect of the job was identifying subtle flavor components and hunting for off-flavors to create a better product. I look forward to bringing this attention to detail to a career in Development.
